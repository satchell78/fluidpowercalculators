How to Use These Tools

  • Define Load: Enter your required Force (lbs) and Stroke (inches). The tools will calculate the minimum theoretical bore area needed.
  • Input Pressure: Use System Pressure for hydraulics and Inlet Pressure for pneumatics.
  • Select Configuration: Choose your cylinder type (e.g., NFPA Tie-Rod). The calculator will automatically snap to the next largest standard industry bore size.
  • Refine Rods: For designs where rod buckling or high-speed retraction is a concern, use the Rod Oversize dropdown to adjust actual values.
  • Analyze Speed: Provide a time or flow rate to see calculated IPM (inches per minute) and required pump/valve capacity.
